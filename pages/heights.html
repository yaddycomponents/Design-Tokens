Seed + formula
controlHeight = 32px ★
XS = ×0.75 · SM = ×0.875 · MD = ×1 · LG = ×1.25 · XL = ×1.5
All derived tokens
controlHeight = 32 ★ controlHeightXS = 24 controlHeightSM = 28 controlHeightLG = 40 controlHeightXL = 48
Why this matters
All interactive controls — buttons, inputs, selects, date pickers — share the same height scale. Changing controlHeight in ConfigProvider resizes all at once.
